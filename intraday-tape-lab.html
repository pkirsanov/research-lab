Methodology (short). VWAP = Σ(typical·vol)/Σvol per session (typical = (H+L+C)/3), reset at the session open; σ bands from the volume-weighted dispersion of typical price around VWAP. The session volume profile buckets each bar's volume at its typical price and splits it up/down by whether the bar closed ≥ its open (the same convention TradingView uses) — this is a proxy, not aggressor-sided flow. POC = highest-volume bucket; the value area is the 70% of volume around it (VAH/VAL). Open-vs-value-area compares today's open to the prior session's value area. The control read and session type are heuristic inferences; the analog hit-rate is a descriptive base rate over the fetched window (gated to a minimum sample), never a forecast. 0DTE walls are read from the Options Structure Lab's local snapshot (prior-session OI).
