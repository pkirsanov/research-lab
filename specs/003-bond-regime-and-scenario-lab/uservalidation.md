# User Validation: 003 Bond Regime and Fixed-Income Scenario Lab

**Evidence source:** [report.md](report.md)
**Execution plan:** [scopes.md](scopes.md)

## Goal

Validate that the delivered static Bond Regime workspace separates observed credit, rates, curve, inflation, and source state from user assumptions and modeled sleeve estimates; remains truthful under stale, partial, restricted, or failed data; and gives Simple and Power users one coherent, accessible decision model.

## Journey Steps

1. Open the Bond Regime Lab with a usable cache and confirm observed evidence paints before refresh completion, with source, as-of, retrieval, freshness, adjustment, and rights labels.
2. Inspect JNK/LQD and HYG/LQD, the latest common date, trend/percentile context, duration gap, estimated duration effect, independent confirmation, conflicts, confidence, confirmation, and invalidation.
3. Compare curve level, 10Y-2Y and 10Y-3M horizons, named curve impulse, real yield, derived breakeven, and the separate Duration Posture.
4. Select every named scenario, edit horizon and signed shocks, and verify all seven sleeves expose carry, rate, spread, convexity, total, break-even, rankability, and reliability.
5. Switch Simple and Power repeatedly and confirm regimes, confidence, expression, ranking, assumptions, confirmation, invalidation, and model digest remain coherent.
6. Exercise missing optional macro, stale fund characteristics, failed refresh, invalid numeric input, and oversized shock states without losing valid evidence or receiving numeric substitutes.
7. Inspect provenance and source-rights disclosures, enter one current-tab optional observation, reload, and confirm the raw value and URL were never persisted or published.
8. Navigate both modes by keyboard and screen reader at desktop and mobile widths; inspect text-equivalent charts, contained tables, visible focus, live announcements, reduced motion, canvas pixels, and overlap behavior.
9. Open the registered tool from the index and navigation, inspect its notes/config links, and confirm the normalized Market Brief read contains only derived, permitted fields.
