# User Validation: Palm Springs Rental Market Lab

Evidence will be recorded in [report.md](report.md). Checked boxes below mean the acceptance questions are present in this planning template; they do not mean the unimplemented feature has been exercised or accepted by a human.

## Goal

Confirm that the delivered tool gives a truthful, source-traceable Palm Springs rental-market thesis and deterministic scenario analysis without obscuring uncertainty, definition conflicts, negative economics, or data availability.

## Checklist

- [x] At human acceptance, confirm first paint automatically loads same-origin configuration and research, with exact current, stale, invalid-configuration, invalid-payload, and unavailable wording.
- [x] At human acceptance, confirm Simple leads with thesis, phase, direction, confidence, strongest support, strongest conflict or unknown, and what changes the view before controls and results.
- [x] At human acceptance, change every market and acquisition control and confirm immediate deterministic updates with no research request or change to the agent thesis.
- [x] At human acceptance, confirm amortizing and zero-rate financing remain finite, gross yield remains pre-expense, and negative pre-tax cash flow remains signed and explicit.
- [x] At human acceptance, compare paid occupancy with available-night occupancy and legal supply with active OTA listings; confirm definitions remain separate and no aggregate or silent conversion appears.
- [x] At human acceptance, inspect material claims and confirm complete source, period, geography, population, rights, limitation, and classification detail with exact dialog focus return.
- [x] At human acceptance, compare Simple and Power on desktop and mobile; confirm one thesis/result identity, readable controls, no body overflow or overlap, keyboard operation, and chart/table parity.
- [x] At human acceptance, confirm observed, forecast, inference, modeled output, conflict, stale, unavailable, and negative states remain distinguishable without color or pointer-only interaction.
- [x] At human acceptance, confirm the owner read preserves current, stale, and unavailable states, omits invalid numeric fields, and deep-links the owning tool from Market Brief without copied research or equations.
- [x] At human acceptance, review the manual research packet and confirm all six categories were actually researched or explicitly unresolved, source rights were respected, the validator ran, and the payload remained uncommitted for review.
