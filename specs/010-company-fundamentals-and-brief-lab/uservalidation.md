# User Validation: Company Fundamentals And Adaptive Brief Lab

Planning authority: [spec.md](spec.md), [design.md](design.md), and the [scopes](scopes.md) plan. Execution evidence is recorded in the single-file feature report at [report.md](report.md).

## Journey Goal

A research user can select MSFT, CMG, or JPM and receive one source-qualified company dossier whose statements, contextual diagnostics, company-specific drivers, linked scenarios, adaptive brief, export, and distributed owner read remain auditable, company-appropriate, and explicitly bounded under incomplete or conflicting evidence.

## Success Signal

Simple explains current fundamental direction, resilience, material drivers, what changed, model effects, risks, and freshness. Detailed traces every material number/claim, period, restatement, formula, assumption, comparison, and brief decision. Both modes and Feature 002 consume the same accepted publication and preserve distinct statement, model, brief, and market clocks.

## Checklist

- [x] This checklist is initialized from all 32 scenario contracts and the eight-scope plan; this row records planning structure only and does not certify delivered behavior.
- [ ] Company identity, security/share class, fiscal context, accounting basis, source clocks, mappings, rights, and every material fact/claim remain traceable through one coherent hash-valid publication.
- [ ] Missing, absent-from-source, explicit zero, unavailable, stale, conflicted, rejected, and restated remain distinct; dependent outputs are withheld without hiding independent valid facts.
- [ ] Controlled Node ingestion requires a privately supplied non-secret `SEC_USER_AGENT`, accepts only approved SEC/issuer paths, emits secret-safe receipts, stages complete generations, and changes current pointers last.
- [ ] Annual, quarter, YTD, trailing, instant, amended, recast, 52/53-week, currency, split-basis, and conflict states preserve period meaning and never create an invalid comparison.
- [ ] Statement identities, cash roll-forwards, per-share bases, and precision intervals expose exact inputs/differences/qualifications and block only dependent conclusions.
- [ ] Derived metrics expose formulas and inputs; the five resilience checks keep raw/contextual results side by side and never create a universal score or advice conclusion.
- [ ] Preferred stock omission is not zero, retained earnings shows distribution context, and buybacks show issuance/SBC/net share effects before interpretation.
- [ ] Archetype and KPI policies change priority/applicability without changing shared facts; unclassified companies inherit no convenient lens; peer statistics contain only comparable observations.
- [ ] MSFT prioritizes sourced software/cloud/backlog/capex/depreciation/cash-conversion/dilution questions, CMG preserves unit/lease/treasury context, and JPM uses bank credit/liquidity/capital questions without an industrial debt verdict.
- [ ] Ordinary-company and bank model graphs use explicit accepted assumptions, recompute one dependency graph, preserve scenario/history lineage, and block cycles/non-finite/invalid branches without hidden balancing values.
- [ ] Evidence refresh cannot rebase user assumptions; proposal arrival is inert; accept/edit confirmation creates one child revision; rejection remains recorded without changing the active scenario.
- [ ] Simple and Detailed plus all six workspaces share one accepted state and make zero view-triggered source requests or alternate computations.
- [ ] Keyboard, focus, ARIA, live-region, non-color, chart/table, 320/768/1440, zoom, text-spacing, and reduced-motion behavior remains complete without body overflow, overlap, or clipping.
- [ ] Material filings and KPI changes lead the adaptive brief through deterministic company-specific ranking, while management commentary remains a claim and repeated generic headlines gain no volume advantage.
- [ ] Rumor/news/sentiment/market/macro evidence retains its own class/window/source and cannot create a reported fact, redate statements, or change an accepted scenario without a user decision.
- [ ] Stale/partial/conflicted evidence preserves the last valid cutoff and constrains claims; duplicate/confirmation/immaterial evidence produces one explicit unchanged outcome without narrative or history duplication.
- [ ] Export and `FundamentalsToolRead/v1` match the accepted on-screen generation, retain all clocks/classes/conflicts/limitations, and contain no credentials, restricted bodies, private scenarios, holdings, or personalized action.
- [ ] Feature 002 consumes the committed owner read once, preserves owner status/clocks/disagreement, and cannot recompute facts, change the archetype, apply a proposal, or promote price movement to fresh fundamentals.
- [ ] Live MSFT, CMG, and JPM acquisition produces current source-qualified validated publications, the next scheduled brief process can disposition them, and every existing Research Lab registry/navigation/brief/provider consumer remains green.
- [ ] International/IFRS normalization, commercial-provider activation, personalized advice/trading, and automatic proposal application are absent from active configuration, runtime behavior, public data, and acceptance claims.
