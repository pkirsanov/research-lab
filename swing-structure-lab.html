Methodology (short). Moving averages are simple 20/50/200-day means; alignment = their stacking order vs price. The composite volume profile buckets each day's volume at its typical price ((H+L+C)/3), split up/down by the day's close vs open — POC = highest-volume price; HVN shelves tend to hold, LVN gaps tend to be crossed fast. Market structure reads swing pivots (HH/HL vs LH/LL); pattern detection (double top/bottom, range, trend) is heuristic and each carries a descriptive analog base rate, never a forecast. Accumulation/distribution blends OBV slope, up/down volume and effort-vs-result. The regime is an in-browser Fear & Greed proxy (SPX momentum vs its 125-day MA, VIX vs its 50-day MA, stocks-vs-Treasuries) plus the VIX level — a proxy, not CNN's index. Option magnets are read from the Options Structure Lab's local snapshot (prior-session OI).
